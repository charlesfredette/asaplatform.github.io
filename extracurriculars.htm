The Toolbox

The Toolbox is a project aimed in creating opportunities and helping high school and college students succeed. Cofounded by my friend Abdurrahim Birik and I, we work alongside teachers to create products such as textbooks and memory aids to help students ace their classes and learn in an effective way. We differentiate ourselves from other corporations that make textbooks because all of the content is student-oriented and verified by students.

We are currently working on making the content and developing the eToolbox, an AI-based online platform that adapts exercises and content at the student's pace.

Vertex Investment Club

The Vertex Investment Club is an international community for young investors. The Clubâ€™s mission is to unite teenagers with the main goal of learning together about investing. We hold both weekly and monthly meetings to discuss potential investments, start-up opportunities and learning material with our members from Canada, the United States and India. I initially founded the Club at my secondary school, the first of its kind in the Montreal area, but decided to bring it to the next level.

The Club is currently working on its expansion plan to reach more prospective members and creating educative content to teach investment basics to beginners to developing investment strategies to the more advanced ones.

We are also participating in various investment simulations and competitions, ranging from Wharton's KWHS Investment Competition, in which we finished in the 95th percentile in 2016, to John Molson's School of Business's JMSX simulation, where we were the youngest team to ever participate.
